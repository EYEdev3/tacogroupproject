Our Story

Four friends:Erica, Francine, Janet and Juana moved from Chicago to the city that never sleeps New York City. We searched for our favorite dish and were unable to locate any Korean Tacos in their new city. So they came together and started as NYC's very first and most popular Korean Taco truck. Tangy Tacos in Mid-Town Manhattan. We serve a delicious combination of Korean flavors with a Mexican twist all made from scratch with our own recipes. Six years later, and three additional locations in Brooklyn,Bronx and Queens.

Giving Back

Tangy Tacos pledges 1% of all gross sales to local environmental, food and youth based non-profit organizations. Through this initiative Tangy Tacos has made 11 donations to 9 charities and non-profit organizations in the NYC area which are having a tremendous impact raising awareness and alleviating local environmental issues, supporting community farms , providing access to food, and empowering youth through experiential learning, outdoor education, and leadership development. Tangy Tacos looks forward to supporting more locally run organizations and cultivating new meaningful relationships. Visit our Community Giving Blog for more information on the recipients of Tangy Tacos's 1% program.
